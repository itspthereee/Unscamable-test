Place your PNG icons here:

Required (recommended) sizes:
- icon16.png   (16x16) — small toolbar resolutions
- icon32.png   (32x32) — toolbar on HiDPI
- icon48.png   (48x48) — extensions page
- icon128.png  (128x128) — Chrome extension management / store

Notes:
- Chrome requires PNG (not SVG) for manifest icons.
- Filenames must match the paths in ../manifest.json.
- You can design one master 128x128 and downscale to smaller sizes.

After adding files, reload the extension from chrome://extensions.
